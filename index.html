DEPTH 02 / 09 — ABOUT
About Me

The developer behind the interface

I'm a 13-year-old O-Levels student with a deep interest in full-stack web development and interface design. What started as curiosity about how websites work turned into building complete applications — from database schema to animated front-ends.

I work primarily in the MERN stack — MongoDB, Express, React, and Node — alongside TypeScript and Supabase, and I care just as much about how something feels to use as how it's built underneath.

My goal is to keep building real, functional products while sharpening both my engineering and design instincts — with an eye on freelance work, internships, and eventually a career at the intersection of development and design.
